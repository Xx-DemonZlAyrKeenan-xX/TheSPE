The Stanford Prison Experiment

By Keenan McKibben

The Stanford Prison experiment took place over 40 years ago on August 15-21st, 1971. Its original intention was to study the "psychological effects of authority and powerlessness in a prison environment." By the end of the experiment there was another conclusion to gather. This is that evil can be brought out of a seemingly good person from circumstance, without them even realizing they're doing something horribly wrong.


The Setting


Stanford University is located in California, and houses this experiment. Carried out August 15-21, 1971 in the basement of Jordan Hall
